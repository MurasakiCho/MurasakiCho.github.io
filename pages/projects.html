Mobile Budget App

iOS/Android App for Personal Budgeting We decided to build a cutting edge budgeting app for improving people's lives. You could not only have a dashboard of your finances with ML insights, you could have the app let you know if you are on track with your budget or not, tell you where your spending your money and when, and predict your blunders! Many don't tread with caution or wisdom and lose the money game. One could have cutting edge advice to actually stick to your plan extracted from the experts. This would be a powerful app that combines classical budgeting tools with expert advice and human behavioral psychology. This could be more than just a class project, it could be used by everyday people to better navigate uncertainty in the capitalist landscape.
